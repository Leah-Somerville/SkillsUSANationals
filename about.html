About Us

Our community focuses on bringing together veterans. After our founder, Ben, met a local homeless veteran, Mike, he was inspried to create a beauiful, affordable community for veterans. Unlike many places, Salveo Communitas accepts government vouchers and lowering rent prices. Not only do we have affordable housing but also a community center to host social events and support groups. Our local dog park gives veterans with service animals another outlet to connect and build their community.
